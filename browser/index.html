About Me

I am Dhanushka Bandara, a Front-End Software Engineer with over 5 years of experience
specializing in Angular, .NET, and modern JavaScript frameworks. Passionate about designing scalable, user-focused web applications, I excel in crafting responsive, high-performance solutions that prioritize exceptional UI/UX. Proficient in agile development, I have contributed to diverse projects, from childcare management platforms to production tracking solutions, enhancing functionality and streamlining workflows. My technical expertise spans RESTful APIs, database optimization, and cross-functional collaboration.
As a dedicated professional and Canadian Permanent Resident, I am driven to create impactful solutions that align with client needs and exceed expectations.
